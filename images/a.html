Top Engineering & Technology Institute in Meerut Uttar Pradesh

Founded in 2007, Dewan VS Institute of Engineering & Technology in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, is committed to fostering engineers through academic excellence and cutting-edge learning methods.

The Dewan VS Institute of Engineering and Technology is one of India's finest educational institutions and provides a seamless stream of highly skilled talent to the country and abroad.

We offer various engineering and technological courses, like B.Tech., MBA, and MCA, in collaboration with industry professionals to deliver in-demand skills. We ensure our students have all the practical abilities they’ll need to fulfil industry requirements. This practise has made us the top B.Tech. institution in Meerut. Spread around 75 acres and surrounded by lavish greenery, our college premises are well-equipped with advanced computer laboratories, spacious classrooms, and hi-tech conference rooms. We also have PD Labs, a fully stocked library, workshops to encourage recreational activities, and a sports ground to keep the sporting spirit alive in our students. We are affiliated with AKTU, Lucknow, and accredited with AICTE.

Dewan VS Institute of Engineering & Technology, a name that has gained recognition for itself in the field of engineering and technology education, has emerged as the top choice for students from India and the SAARC region looking for an affordable, top-quality higher education. Our programmes have been thoughtfully created with the assistance of top industry professionals to expose students to cutting-edge research, innovation, and quality education. We deliver exceptional education to our students, whom we view as distinct individuals with diverse interests and aspirations, while continuously improving our teaching strategies.

MHRD AFFILIATION

Education plays a significant and remedial role in balancing the socio-economic fabric of the Country. Since citizens of India are its most valuable resource, our billion-strong nation needs the nurture and care in the form of basic education to achieve a better quality of life. This warrants an all-round development of building strong foundations in education.

AICTE AFFILIATION

In accordance with the provisions of the AICTE Act (1987), for the first five years after its inception in 1988, the Minister for Human Resource Development, the Government of India, was the Chairman of the Council. The first full-time Chairman was appointed on July 2, 1993 and the Council was reconstituted in March 1994 term of three years.
