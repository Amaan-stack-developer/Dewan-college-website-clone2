DEWAN INCUBATION & INNOVATION FORUM

Dewan Incubation & Innovation Forum serves as a dynamic hub for aspiring entrepreneurs and creative thinkers within the college community. Designed to inspire innovation and support early-stage ventures, the centre provides students, researchers, and budding entrepreneurs with the resources and guidance needed to transform original ideas into impactful startups.

The Centre has received approval from the Government of India‚Äôs Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) and the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME). It is registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013.

The initiative aims to cultivate a culture of entrepreneurship by offering mentorship, technical support, and infrastructure in areas like Software Development, Data Analytics, Web Designing, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, ED Tech, E-Commerce, Cloud Computing Services.
