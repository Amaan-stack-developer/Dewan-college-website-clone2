DEWAN INCUBATION & INNOVATION FORUM

Dewan Incubation & Innovation Forum serves as a dynamic hub for aspiring entrepreneurs and creative thinkers within the college community. Designed to inspire innovation and support early-stage ventures, the centre provides students, researchers, and budding entrepreneurs with the resources and guidance needed to transform original ideas into impactful startups.
