Our Institute

Dewan VS Institute of Engineering & Technology is on an enthralling path of growth and development since its establishment in 2007. The Institute owns the lush green campus on 77 acres of land. It is located on Delhi– Mussoorie Highway near the junction point of Delhi–Meerut Expressway & proposed Meerut South Station of Rapid Rail Transport System.

The institute is affiliated to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. It offers B.Tech, MBA and MCA programs across multiple disciplines.
